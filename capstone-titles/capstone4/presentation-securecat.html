Section 1.0 · Context & Core Failures
§ 02 / 06

Vulnerabilities in Manual Testing Workflows

Institutional Reality
"A proctor glances at a student ID card at the testing room door. Weeks later, anomalies emerge in the scoring spreadsheets with no trail."
At ISPSC Tagudin, college admission testing governs the entry of hundreds of applicants. Currently, scheduling relies on paper logs and separate spreadsheets. Exam room identity verification relies purely on visual ID checks. Score modifications go untraced. Applicants wait in complete informational isolation.
ispsc_manual_operations.log
[WARN] Duplicate sched for room IT-101. Sheet overwritten.
[WARN] Applicant David Datu: scores modified. Who changed Abstract Reasoning?
[ERROR] Manual verification failed. No audit log found for score commits.
guidance@ispsc-tagudin:~$ echo "Data integrity risk detected"
01
Identity Verification Gaps
Exam room check-in is purely visual. Impersonation risks are high. No digital link exists connecting the physical examinee to the registered online profile.
Impact: Entry gate vulnerability
02
Disconnected Data & No Audit
Rosters exist in separate files. Score modifications occur without audit trails or cryptographic non-repudiation constraints.
Impact: Severe data integrity risk

Research Objectives
